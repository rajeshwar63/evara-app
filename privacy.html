Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Evara ("we", "our", "us") is a product of Sipsea Beverages, a registered business in Hyderabad, Telangana, India (GSTIN: 36AFMFS5330L1ZJ). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Evara WhatsApp-based document organizer and reminder service ("Service").

1. Information we collect

When you use Evara, we collect and process the following types of information:

  • Phone number: Your WhatsApp phone number, used to identify your account and communicate with you.
  • Documents and media: Photos, PDFs, and documents you forward to Evara for organizing. These are stored securely on our servers so you can retrieve them later.
  • Text notes: Any text messages you send to Evara for saving as notes.
  • Document metadata: AI-extracted information from your documents, including names, dates, amounts, organizations, and tags. This metadata enables search and retrieval.
  • Reminders: Reminder text, dates, and times you set through the Service.
  • Usage data: Search queries, message timestamps, and processing logs to improve the Service.

2. How we use your information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the Evara Service:

  • To receive, process, tag, and store your documents and notes
  • To enable you to search and retrieve your stored documents
  • To send you reminders at the times you specify
  • To improve our AI tagging accuracy and search relevance
  • To communicate service updates and important notices

3. How we store your data

  • Document files (photos, PDFs) are stored encrypted on Cloudflare R2 cloud storage with servers globally distributed for fast access.
  • Document metadata (text, tags, dates) is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database hosted on Supabase.
  • All data is isolated per user — no user can access another user's documents, notes, or reminders.
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest.

4. AI processing

When you send a document to Evara, we use artificial intelligence (Google Gemini) to extract text and metadata from your documents. This processing happens in real-time to provide instant tagging and categorization. The AI provider does not store your documents beyond the processing request.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We share data only with:

  • Service providers: Cloud infrastructure providers (Cloudflare, Supabase, Google) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • WhatsApp/Meta: Messages are transmitted through WhatsApp's platform, subject to WhatsApp's own privacy policy.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.

6. Data retention and deletion

  • Your documents and data are retained as long as your account is active.
  • You can delete any individual document at any time by messaging "delete" followed by the document name.
  • You can request complete account deletion by messaging "delete my account" to Evara. We will delete all your data within 30 days.
  • If your account is inactive for 12 months, we may delete your data after sending a reminder notification.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all documents and data stored in your Evara account
  • Delete any or all of your stored documents and notes
  • Request a copy of all your data
  • Opt out of the Service at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

8. Children's privacy

Evara is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through a WhatsApp message or by updating the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
