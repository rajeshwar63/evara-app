FAQ

Is my data safe?
Your documents are stored on encrypted cloud servers. Only you can access them through your WhatsApp number. We never share your data with anyone.
Do I need to download any app?
Nope! Evara works 100% inside WhatsApp. Just message us and start saving documents. Nothing to install.
What languages does it understand?
Evara understands English, Hindi, Hinglish, and can read documents in any language. Search also works in Hinglish — type "mera aadhaar dikhao" and it works!
How do I get Pro for free?
Just share Evara with 1 friend. When they message Evara and mention your name or number, both of you get Pro access for a full year. Refer more friends to extend it further!
What happens to my documents if I stop using Evara?
Your documents stay safe in your account. You can come back anytime and access them. We never delete your data unless you ask us to.
