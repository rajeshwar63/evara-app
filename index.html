How it works

Four things Evara does

1

Pin Evara on WhatsApp

Save our number, send "Hi", and pin the chat. Evara is now your personal assistant — always at the top.

2

Forward what matters

Got a medical bill in a family group? Long-press, forward to Evara. Received a PDF insurance policy? Forward it. Took a photo of a receipt? Send it directly. Photos, PDFs, documents — Evara reads them all.

3

Get it back when you need it

Type "apollo bills" or "PAN card" or "insurance policy" — Evara sends you the actual document back with a summary. Your personal search engine, inside WhatsApp.

4

Set reminders in plain language

"Remind me to pay electricity bill tomorrow 10AM" — done. "Remind me to renew insurance on Dec 1st" — done. Evara pings you on WhatsApp right on time. No app, no notification settings, just a message.
